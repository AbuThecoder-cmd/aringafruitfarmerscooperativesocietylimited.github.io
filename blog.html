Nursery Bed Seedlings

Do you know that you can keep adequate nursery bed seedlings during dry seasons?

This is what you should do:

  • Daily watering of seedlings using a watering can.
  • Keep better shades for the seedlings to avoid them drying completely.
  • Spray with necessary pesticides to keep away pests.
  • Harvest ready seedlings for planting in time.
