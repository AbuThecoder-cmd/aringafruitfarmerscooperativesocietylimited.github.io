Membership

Membership of the cooperative shall be open to all local mango farmers in Yumbe and West Nile region.
The membership shall be maintained as being current by paying the annual subscription, development fees, registration fees, and securing a minimum of two shares.

Strategic Focus Areas

Strengthening Institutional Capacity

Strengthening of commodity associations, formation and capacitation of collection centres, and full staffing of the cooperative with permanent staff.

Advocacy and Stakeholder Engagement

Farmer organization, development of policies and procedures.

ICT Integration

Ensuring ICT tools affordability to its general membership, empower the resource-poor farmers with improved access to information, input suppliers,
financers, and market linkages.

Member Services

Intensified member mobilization and registration, riding on a broad range of good quality and needed services. Maintaining close contact with the members.

Women, Youth, and Climate Change

Investment in youth and women empowerment. Investment in agricultural development is required to ensure sustainable food and nutrition security in the face of changing climate.
